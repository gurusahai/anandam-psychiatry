FAQs

Common Questions

Clear answers to reduce uncertainty before your first visit.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes. Telepsychiatry and telepsychology options are available. Book via phone/WhatsApp or the form below.

Should I see a psychiatrist or a psychologist?

For medical assessment and medication review, start with the psychiatrist. For therapy/counselling, start with a psychologist. If unsure, message us and we'll guide you.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. We treat patient information with strict confidentiality.

What if I need urgent help?

If you feel unsafe or at risk of self-harm, contact local emergency services immediately or go to the nearest hospital emergency department.
